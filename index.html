About Us

Formed in December 2019. The idea was to set up a small league of PES players that wanted to enjoy a more fair and realistic style of game in contrast to the problems with regards to cheaters, time wasters, silly tactics and exploits often found in pes online modes.


With 10 or so members we amalgamated with PES FC who had a large community of members for teamplay. Over the next months we expanded with over 50 members and three leagues. As we evolved we brought in new aspects and ideas. Such as our very own commentators for live stream games.


Squad restrictions, pass assist level 1 and removal of certain star players means a more balanced game overall. Not only are games more enjoyable theres also lots of fun in the squad building and transfer market as well as the opportunity to face different players rather than same ones you always face online. Auctions and cups and goal of month competion add to the fun. Theres always something going on. Mini 2V2 comps and teamplay nights etc.


Occasionaly someone may leave the group so worth signing into discord and join waiting list if interested. We usually play betweeen 3 to 5 games per week.
